Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question: What should I bring for a river rafting trip?

Answer: We recommend bringing the following items:

  • Quick-drying clothing
  • Swimsuit
  • Waterproof shoes or sandals
  • Sunscreen and a hat
  • Sunglasses with a strap
  • Towel and a change of clothes
  • Water bottle
  • Waterproof camera (optional)

Question: Do I need prior rafting experience?

Answer: No prior experience is necessary! Our trips are designed for all skill levels, and our professional guides will provide a safety briefing and instructions before the trip.

Question: Is there an age or weight limit for river rafting?

Answer: Yes, participants must be at least 8 years old. There is no strict weight limit, but participants should be in good physical health. Please contact us if you have any specific concerns.

Question: What if I can’t swim?

Answer: While it’s helpful to know how to swim, it’s not a requirement. All participants will be provided with a life jacket and safety gear. Our guides are trained to handle various situations to ensure your safety.

Question: What happens if the weather is bad?

Answer: Safety is our top priority. We monitor weather conditions closely and will make a decision based on safety. In the event of dangerous weather, we will reschedule your trip or provide a refund.

Question: Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Answer: Yes, you can bring your own snacks and non-alcoholic beverages. We also provide water and light refreshments on our trips. Please avoid bringing glass containers.

Question: How do I make a reservation?

Answer: You can book your trip online through our website, or you can call our office to make a reservation. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak season.

Question: What is your cancellation policy?

Answer: Cancellations made more than 48 hours in advance will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the trip will be subject to a fee. Please refer to our full cancellation policy on our website for more details.

Question: What should I do with my valuables during the trip?

Answer: We recommend leaving valuables at home or in your vehicle. We provide secure storage for small items like keys and phones, but we are not responsible for any lost or damaged items.

Question: Are there any health restrictions?

Answer: Participants should be in good health and free from any conditions that could be aggravated by physical activity. Pregnant women and individuals with heart conditions or severe asthma are advised not to participate. Please consult with your doctor if you have any health concerns.
